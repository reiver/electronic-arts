Electronic Arts

One of the largest video game companies in the world.

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Electronic Arts (often shortened to just EA) is one of the largest video game in the world — the second largest video game company in the U.S. and Europe. EA is known for making Star Wars, FIFA, Apex Legends, Titanfall, Command & Conquer, Need for Speed, NHL, and many many other video games.

Experience

I did work twice for Electronic Arts (EA).

Both times at the EA campus in Burnaby (Canada). The EA office in Burnaby campus the most expensive and most beautiful EA campuses! It also sometimes gets called EA Sports, EA Canada, EA Burnaby, or EA Vancouver.

The first time I did work for them I did it as a Senior Software Engineer. The last time I did work for them I did it as a Principal Software Engineer.

The first time I was at EA, I created one of their core platform technologies for them twice. (And, twice because when new senior management comes in, they often like to throw out what the previous senior management did and do it all over again under their watch, so it becomes one of their accomplishments.)

The last time I was at EA, I again created a core platform technologies (for a third time), and solved some of EA's major scaling problems. This was part of one of rare advanced development teams inside of EA.
